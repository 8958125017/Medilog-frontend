Features designed for you

Medilocks is a decentralized infrastructure to store Electronic Health Records which are provably accurate.

Interoperability

Empower trusted, safe and auditable sharing of data across global systems, eliminating geographic and system boundaries.

Quicker Diagnosis

Patient’s Clinical and Diagnosis immutable history is accessible anytime anywhere, making diagnosis faster.

Immutability

Records once stored can’t be changed. Any record can be easily verified with Zero Knowledge Proof.

Data Hub

Patient Data will be collected from various sources like IOTs, Clinical Information, & Doctor visits are all appended to EHR DB.

Provenance

All Pateint History data on Medilocks carry a verifiably accurate provenance trail. Since the data is tamper-proof, this improves subsequent diagnosis time. 

Medicine Purchases 

Pharmaceutical purchase history and Prescription Management tools help patients receive right medicine in appropriate dosage.
